About Us

The SLSU EE Graduate Tracer System is a comprehensive platform designed to monitor and track the career progression of Electrical Engineering graduates from Southern Luzon State University. This initiative aims to maintain strong connections with our alumni and provide valuable insights into their professional achievements.

Our mission is to foster a vibrant alumni community, celebrate their successes, and strengthen the bond between graduates and the institution that shaped their careers.
